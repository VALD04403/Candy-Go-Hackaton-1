Rule's Game

It's simple !

Like Pokemon Go, you walk in the street for find some candies for halloween's season; Candy-Go allows you to locate on a map candies avaible around you. You have to zoom on the map to find candies, you click on it and it is add to your Candy-box, each one give you 50 energy points, you start the game with basic candies like the Tic-Tac. You can change energy points in trophies, for example Dragolo worth 352 energy points. The goal is to gather the maximum of trophies.

Good luck young hunter!
